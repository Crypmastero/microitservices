Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request immediate support?

Use the booking panel at the top, call our toll-free number, or submit the contact form below. Remote sessions usually begin within minutes.

Which antivirus brands do you support?

We support major antivirus vendors including Norton, McAfee, Bitdefender, Kaspersky, ESET, Avast, AVG, Trend Micro, Sophos and more. If you use a niche product, contact us and we will check compatibility.

Do you offer on-site visits?

Yes — we provide on-site technician visits in covered areas. Remote resolution is usually faster and more cost-effective.

Are remote sessions secure?

All remote sessions are encrypted and possible authorization steps are explained before connecting. We never request passwords without proper verification.
