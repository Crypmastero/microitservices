Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my wireless printer?

Open Wi-Fi settings on the printer, select your network, and enter your password. We can guide you remotely if needed.

Why is my printer offline?

This happens due to network disconnection or driver errors. We can help fix that remotely.

How do I install a new printer driver?

Download the latest driver from the brand’s website or contact us for quick installation help.

Can I print from my phone?

Yes, most modern printers support mobile printing via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.

How do I fix slow printing?

Updating drivers and clearing print queues often resolves the issue.

Does MicroTech support HP printers?

Yes, we provide complete HP printer setup, repair, and maintenance services.

What causes paper jams?

Paper misalignment or debris in rollers can cause jams. We can guide you step-by-step.

Can you fix print quality issues?

Yes, we help resolve faded print, color mismatches, and alignment errors.

How do I refill my ink cartridges?

We can assist with brand-specific ink or toner refill procedures.

Do you provide remote support?

Yes, we offer 24/7 remote printer troubleshooting and repair assistance.
