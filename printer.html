Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my printer show offline?+
Printer offline can be due to network drop, IP change, print spooler issues or driver conflicts. Restart devices, check network, and reinstall drivers if needed.
How do I connect a printer to Wi-Fi?+
Use the printer's wireless setup wizard or WPS, or connect temporarily via USB and use the manufacturer's software to configure Wi-Fi settings.
Do you provide on-site support?+
Yes — we provide remote and on-site support depending on the complexity and location.
